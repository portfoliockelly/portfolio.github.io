From Prototype to finished product.

 

This Game over the course of a year transformed from the original plans and game mechanics, to suit the client and designer needs. As the work progressed and prototypes where brought forward to present to the client, often the clients need would change or something would be brought forward that was unknown previously. This would affect the game design from the artwork down to the core program and game mechanics. New technical risks would then be solved, and the new design would be implemented for the next milestone until complete and ready for presentation.

The video below was the finished product.

Note: Ownership of this game is reserved by Glasgow Caledonian University. This was designed to be a teaching aid for waste management on building sites.
